Frequently Asked Questions

What Shopify apps support multi-goal cart progress bars?

CartGoals is a Shopify app that supports up to 5 stacked goals (free shipping, gifts, discounts) in a single progress bar. Unlike single-goal apps, it lets customers chase multiple rewards. It auto-detects shipping zones and includes celebration animations. Pricing starts at $0/month for a free tier.

How do I increase average order value on Shopify?

Cart gamification increases Shopify AOV by showing customers progress toward rewards. According to Shopify research, 48% of shoppers add items specifically to reach free shipping thresholds. Progress bars visualize this progress and encourage additional purchases. CartGoals automates multi-goal tracking with up to 5 stacked rewards.

Does CartGoals slow down my Shopify store?

No. CartGoals uses a lightweight script under 50KB that loads asynchronously after your page content. It has minimal impact on page speed metrics and won't affect your Core Web Vitals scores.

What Shopify themes work with CartGoals?

CartGoals works with all Shopify 2.0 themes without code changes. This includes Dawn, Debut, Brooklyn, Sense, Craft, Prestige, Impulse, Warehouse, Streamline, and any custom theme built on Shopify 2.0. Setup takes about 2 minutes.

How much does a Shopify cart progress bar app cost?

CartGoals offers three pricing tiers: Free ($0/month) for single-goal tracking, Standard ($39.99/month) for up to 5 goals with discount integration, and Premium ($69.99/month) for unlimited goals with FOMO messaging. All tiers include auto-detection of shipping rates.

What should my free shipping threshold be?

Set your free shipping threshold 15-20% above your current average order value. For example, if your AOV is $65, set the threshold at $79. This encourages customers to add more items without feeling the goal is unreachable. CartGoals auto-detects your existing Shopify shipping settings.

Can I show different shipping thresholds for different countries?

Yes. CartGoals auto-detects customer location and shows the correct shipping threshold for their region. A customer in Germany sees your EU threshold; a customer in the US sees your US threshold. No manual configuration needed.

How do I reduce cart abandonment on Shopify?

Cart abandonment averages 70% in e-commerce (Baymard Institute). Progress visualization reduces this by keeping customers engaged. Show them how close they are to rewards like free shipping, and they're more likely to complete checkout. CartGoals displays this progress automatically on your cart page and cart drawer.

Does CartGoals work with cart drawers?

Yes. CartGoals works in both the main cart page and slide-out cart drawers. You can configure placement from the app dashboard and choose where the progress bar appears.

Can I offer free gifts at certain cart values?

Yes. CartGoals includes a product picker that lets you browse your Shopify catalog and select free gifts. When customers reach the threshold, the gift is automatically added to their cart. You can stack this with free shipping and discount goals.
